About CivicAI

Empowering Indian citizens with AI-driven legal insights to foster transparency and accountability.

Our Mission

The Right to Information (RTI) Act is a powerful tool for democracy, but navigating its legalities can be daunting. Our mission is to bridge this gap by making expert-level legal advice accessible to everyone.

We believe that every citizen deserves to know their rights and how to exercise them effectively without needing expensive legal counsel for basic queries.

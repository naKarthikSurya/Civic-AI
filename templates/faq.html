Frequently Asked Questions

What is RTI?

RTI stands for Right to Information. It is an act of the Parliament of India which sets out the rules and procedures regarding citizens' right to information.

How can this AI help me?

Our AI assistant can help you understand if your query is valid under RTI, find relevant past judgments, and draft a formal application for you to submit.

Is the generated draft legally binding?

No. The draft is a template based on your inputs. You should review it carefully and consult a legal expert if necessary before submission.

Does this service file the RTI for me?

No, we only help you draft the application. You need to submit it either online (rtionline.gov.in) or via post to the respective Public Information Officer.
